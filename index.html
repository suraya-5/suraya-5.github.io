SQL Game of Thrones Project

This SQL project demonstrates my ability to design and work with relational databases. I created a Game of Thrones-themed database to practice building tables, managing relationships, and writing queries for analysis. The project is a fun way for me to combine my interests with technical skills while simulating real-world financial and transactional data management.

Tools That I Used

  • Python
  • Lists and dictionaries to store and process data
  • Loops and calculations for aggregating numbers
  • Text-based charts for a quick visual overview

What I Did

  1. Calculated revenue per order – multiplying units sold by unit price for each transaction.
  2. Summarized overall sales – total revenue and average revenue per order.
  3. Analyzed revenue by product – to see which products were bringing in the most cash.
  4. Analyzed revenue by customer – which characters are the biggest spenders?
  5. Checked revenue by month – spotting trends over time.
  6. Created text-based charts – because even simple visualizations help tell the story.
